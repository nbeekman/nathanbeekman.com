SchoolSet

Dilemma: Develop a learning platform where university students and teachers can communicate efficiently no matter what the teaching and learning style is.

Answer: We interviewed students and created personas based on those. We then laid out task models based on touch points of the personas. Finally, we designed a mid-fidelity prototype after running through usability testing.
