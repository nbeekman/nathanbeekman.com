Faculty/Lecturer Peer Review

Again, to speed up the process of a paper form, I created an electronic form to route through the HR Info System. The form is for the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences (CLAS) at The University of Iowa. It is a Peer Review for all faculty and lecturers at the CLAS that is filled out by fellow faculty members and then is rated on by the college's Dean. With the ratings, only the Dean and his staff can see them. After the Dean rates the faculty member, an email is sent to them stating its completion.
