About

I am 25 years old and a graduate of The University of Iowa with a business degree in Management Information Systems. I have around 2 years of professional web development experience. I have had a love for computers since I was young and that love has turned into a passion of creating anything and everything digital.

Tech Experience: PHP, HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL

I worked at The University of Iowa in Human Resources for the Information Management department developing different web applications, tools, and reports for the University's HR website used by employees for personal and administrative use. I have worked on some freelance projects in my spare time as well.

I am currently attending BDW at the University of Colorado-Boulder.
